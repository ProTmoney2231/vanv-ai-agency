How messaging works

Customers may receive SMS messages after they interact with the business, request service, or book an appointment. Message types include appointment confirmations, reminders, reschedule updates, cancellation notices, and follow-up messages related to booked services.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

How users opt in

  • Booking an appointment through the website
  • Speaking with the AI receptionist during a phone call
  • Requesting service or follow-up from the business

Before messages are sent, users are informed that they agree to receive appointment-related SMS messages, that message frequency varies, that message and data rates may apply, and that they can reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help.
