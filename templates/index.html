How users opt in

End users can opt in by:

  • submitting a booking request or service request through the website
  • providing consent during a phone call with the AI receptionist
  • requesting service or follow-up related to an appointment

Before messages are sent, users are informed that appointment-related SMS messages may be sent, message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, and they can reply STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for help.
