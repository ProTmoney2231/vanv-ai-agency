SMS consent and compliance

Vanv AI Agency LLC provides AI receptionist and appointment messaging services for medical spas, dental offices, clinics, and similar service businesses. This page explains who sends the messages, how end users opt in, the types of messages they receive, and how they can opt out.

Sender identity

Sender / brand: Vanv AI Agency LLC
Website: vanvaiagency.com
Use case: AI receptionist, appointment booking, SMS confirmations, reminders, reschedules, cancellations, and service follow-ups.

How end users opt in

  • Submitting a booking request or service request through the website.
  • Providing consent during a phone call or interaction with the AI receptionist for appointment-related messaging.
  • Requesting follow-up about a service, consultation, or appointment.

Before messages are sent, users are informed that appointment-related text messages may be sent, message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, and they can reply STOP to opt out or HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message types

  • Appointment confirmations
  • Appointment reminders
  • Reschedule updates
  • Cancellation notices
  • Service follow-ups related to booked services

Sample messages

Vanv AI Agency: Hi [Customer Name], your [Service] appointment is confirmed for [Date] at [Time]. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Vanv AI Agency: Reminder — you have a [Service] appointment on [Date] at [Time]. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Vanv AI Agency: Your appointment has been rescheduled to [Date] at [Time]. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Vanv AI Agency: Your appointment on [Date] at [Time] has been canceled. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Vanv AI Agency: We received your request and will follow up shortly. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

Opt-out and help

Opt out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.
Help: Reply HELP for help.
Message frequency: Varies by appointment activity.
Rates: Message and data rates may apply.
