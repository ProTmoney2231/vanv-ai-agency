How end users opt in

End users opt in through the website form at https://vanvaiagency.com by entering their name, email, and phone number and manually selecting the unchecked SMS consent checkbox before submitting. The checkbox language states that message frequency varies, message and data rates may apply, reply STOP to opt out, and reply HELP for help. Links to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy are shown next to the checkbox. Consent is not a condition of purchase. We do not use purchased lists, shared consent, or third-party lead lists.
