Privacy Policy — Vanv AI Agency

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Who we are

Vanv AI Agency LLC provides AI receptionist, appointment booking, SMS confirmation, reminder, rescheduling, cancellation, and follow-up tools for medical spas, dental offices, clinics, and similar service businesses. For privacy questions, contact vanvaiagency@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Appointment details
  • Message history related to bookings and customer support
  • Business and calendar connection data needed to provide the service

How we use information

  • Book, confirm, reschedule, and cancel appointments
  • Send SMS confirmations, reminders, and follow-up messages
  • Provide customer support and service updates
  • Prevent errors, abuse, duplicate requests, and service misuse
  • Maintain service reliability and logs

SMS and mobile information

If a customer provides a mobile number and consents to receive messages, we may send appointment-related SMS messages such as confirmations, reminders, reschedule notices, cancellation notices, and support follow-ups.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Recipients can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.

Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

How information is shared

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only with service providers that help us operate the platform, such as hosting, automation, messaging, calendar, and payment vendors, and only as needed to provide the service.

Google and calendar data

When authorized, we may access calendar data to read availability, create bookings, update bookings, and manage appointment records. We do not use calendar data for advertising or resale.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion by emailing vanvaiagency@gmail.com.
  • You may opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • You may request help by replying HELP.
